Cardiology - Street Lane Services
  • This clinic is available to patients from within and outside Street Lane Practice via Choose and Book. It is run by 2 Consultant Cardiologists from Leeds General Infirmary.   • This service requires a referral by your GP
Child health and development - Street Lane Services Paediatrics
  • Consultant paediatricians from local hospitals see patients referred from within Street Lane Practice or from other GP surgeries in Leeds via Choose and Book. Conditions commonly seen include headaches, constipation, funny turns and asthma. There is a full list on our website.
  • This service requires a referral by your GP
Dermatology specialist clinic - Street Lane Services Dermatology
  • We run a specialist clinic including a Consultant Dermatologist, GPs with Special interest in Dermatology and specialist nurses. They see patients referred from within Street Lane Practice. Patients from throughout Leeds can be referred via Choose and Book. Please see the full range of conditions treated and services offered on our Street Lane Practice website
  • This service requires a referral by your GP
Learning disability health check
  • You can attend this service without a referral
Long-Acting Reversible Contraception (LARC - eg IUD or implant)
  • Members of our medical and nursing team can fit coils and contraceptive implants on patients registered at Street Lane Practice.. You can attend this service without a referral
Minor surgery (e.g. removal of moles and skin lesions) - provided in-house
  • We provide a minor surgery service for patients registered at Street Lane Practice. Patients can be referred from other GPs via choose and book as we have 3 GPs with Special Interest in Minor Surgery.
  • We perform the following services:
  • Remove common non-cancerous skin lesions including facial lesions
  • Joint injections - knees, shoulders, hands
  • Ingrowing toenail removal
  • Injection/excision keloid scars.
  • Injection/aspiration of ganglions
  • This service requires a referral by your GP
